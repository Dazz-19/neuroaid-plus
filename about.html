💡 About NeuroAid+

Technology that speaks when you can’t.

Our Mission

At NeuroAid+, our mission is simple but powerful: to empower non-verbal individuals with a voice — through affordable, AI-powered communication tools. We’re creating a bridge between patients and caregivers, one phrase at a time.

Our Story

Inspired by the challenges faced by people with speech impairments — and the high cost of existing AAC solutions — we set out to build something different. Something smarter, more caring, and open to all.

We believe assistive technology shouldn't be a privilege. With the right blend of hardware and software, we’re changing how accessibility is done — from the ground up.

What We Stand For

  • 🧠 Empathy-first Design: We design with real people, not personas.
  • ⚙️ Affordable Innovation: Our open-source mindset keeps costs low.
  • 🤝 Inclusivity: Every language, every user, every need matters.
  • 💬 Real Communication: More than words — we enable expression.

Meet the Team

We’re a passionate group of students, engineers, designers, and dreamers. What brought us together? A shared belief that **tech can heal**, and everyone deserves to be heard.
